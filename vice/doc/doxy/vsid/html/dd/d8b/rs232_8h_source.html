28/*
29 * This is the header for the RS232 emulation.
30 *
31 * The RS232 emulation captures the bytes sent to the RS232 interfaces
32 * available (currently only ACIA 6551, later UART 16550A, std C64,
33 * and Daniel Dallmanns fast RS232 with 9600 Baud).
34 * The characters captured are displayed in a special terminal window.
35 * Characters typed in the terminal window are sent back to the
36 * chip emulations.
37 */
